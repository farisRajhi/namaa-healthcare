# Dental Clinic Patient Intelligence - Saudi Arabia

| Service | Cycle | First Reminder | Escalation | Arabic Name |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Cleaning (prophylaxis) | Every 6 months | 5 months | 7mo, 10mo | تنظيف الاسنان |
| Whitening | 6-12 months (touch-up) | 5 months | 8mo | تبييض الاسنان |
| Veneers | Annual check | 11 months | 13mo | فينير / عدسات الاسنان |
| Orthodontics (braces/aligners) | Monthly during treatment (18-24mo total) | 3 weeks post-last visit | 5 weeks | تقويم الاسنان |
| Implants - post-surgery | 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, then annual | 2 days before each | day-of if missed | زراعة الاسنان |

| Extraction | Implant consult at 2-3 months post-healing | 8 weeks | 12 weeks | خلع الاسنان |

| Pediatric check | Every 6 months | 5 months | 7mo | اسنان الاطفال |

## Saudi Patient Compliance Rates

- **Cleaning**: 30-35% return on schedule (6mo). ~25% return late (7-12mo). ~40% lapse entirely
- **Whitening**: ~50% never return for touch-up. Best compliance when sold as package
- **Implant follow-ups**: Good at 1-week (85%), drops at 1-month (60%), often missed after month 3 (~35%)
- **Orthodontics**: 80% keep monthly appointments (treatment is active/visible)
- **Root canal to crown**: Only ~55% complete the crown within recommended window. HIGH priority re-engagement
- **Insured patients**: ~50% overall compliance vs ~30% uninsured
- **Male vs female**: Women comply ~10-15% higher across all services

## Re-engagement Windows

| Window | Days Overdue | Priority | Suggested Tone | Expected Response Rate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Prime | 0-30 | High value, easy win | Friendly reminder | 25-35% |
| Moderate | 31-90 | Core re-engagement | Gentle urgency, mention time since last visit | 15-25% |
| Late | 91-180 | Needs incentive | Health-focused, offer flexibility | 8-15% |
| Very lapsed | 180+ | Win-back | "We miss you" + special offer or new services | 3-8% |

For root canal patients without crown: treat 0-14 days as prime (tooth is at risk).

## Cross-sell Paths

Use ONLY when clinically appropriate. Never push cosmetic over clinical need.

- Cleaning --> Whitening (natural upsell, mention after prophylaxis)
- Cleaning --> Gum treatment (if perio signs noted in chart)
- Extraction --> Implant consult (after healing period, 2-3 months)
- Root canal --> Crown (mandatory, not optional -- frame as completing treatment)
- Cleaning --> Veneer consultation (only if patient has expressed cosmetic interest)
- Orthodontics completion --> Whitening (common post-braces desire)
- Pediatric check --> Fluoride treatment / sealants

## Event-driven Demand Patterns

### Pre-Eid Al-Fitr (2-4 weeks before)
- PEAK demand: whitening, veneers, cleaning
- Message 3-4 weeks before: "Get your smile ready for Eid"
- ابتسامة العيد (Eid smile) is a known cultural concept

### Pre-Eid Al-Adha
- Similar but slightly lower cosmetic demand than Fitr
- Still strong for cleaning and whitening

### Wedding Season (summer months + post-Eid)
- Veneers, whitening, orthodontic completion
- Target patients with upcoming weddings if known
- عروس (bride) packages common

### Ramadan
- Appointments drop during fasting hours
- Evening clinics see higher traffic
- Pre-Ramadan: push cleaning (oral hygiene during fasting)
- Message: "Start Ramadan with a clean, healthy smile"

### Back to School (late August)
- Pediatric dental checks
- Orthodontic consultations for teens

## Message Timing Rules

- **Cleaning**: First reminder at 5 months. Second at 7 months ("It's been over 6 months..."). Final at 10 months (urgent tone)
- **Post-procedure follow-up**: Reminder 2 days before scheduled follow-up. Same-day if missed
- **Root canal without crown**: Reminder at 2 weeks. Escalate at 3 weeks with clinical urgency ("unprotected tooth at risk of fracture")
- **Implant follow-ups**: Day-before reminder for each scheduled follow-up. If month-3 missed, call within 1 week
- **Orthodontics**: 1 week before monthly appointment. If missed, contact within 3 days (treatment delays extend total time)

## Insurance Considerations

- Insured patients: emphasize "covered by your insurance" / "مغطى بالتأمين"
- Patients near plan expiry: "Use your remaining dental benefits before [date]"
- Uninsured: mention payment plans, package pricing. Never assume inability to pay
- Common Saudi insurers cover cleaning 2x/year, basic restorative. Cosmetic usually excluded
- When insurance covers it, compliance nearly doubles -- always mention coverage

## Communication Preferences

- WhatsApp strongly preferred over SMS (higher open rates, 85%+ vs 40%)
- Arabic default. Switch to English only if patient profile indicates preference
- Avoid messaging during prayer times and late night (after 10pm)
- Friday morning messages perform poorly (pre-Jummah)
- Best send times: Sunday-Thursday, 10am-12pm or 5pm-8pm
- Keep messages under 160 characters for initial outreach. Details in follow-up

## Age-Based Service Recommendations

| Age Group | Key Services | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Children (0-12) | Fluoride treatments, sealants, 6-month checkups | Target parents, not children |
| Teens (13-18) | Orthodontic assessment, wisdom tooth monitoring | Back-to-school campaigns effective |
| Young adults (19-30) | Wisdom teeth extraction, cosmetic (whitening, veneers) | Highest cosmetic demand |
| Adults (31-50) | Gum disease screening, crown/bridge maintenance, implants | Insurance coverage messaging |
| Seniors (50+) | Denture care, dry mouth management, oral cancer screening | Gentle tone, family involvement |

## Scoring Signals by Last Service

Use these to adjust patient scores:

- **Cleaning overdue 6+ months**: HIGH priority (score 60-80). This is the bread-and-butter recall.
- **Root canal without crown (within 4 weeks)**: CRITICAL (score 90+). Unprotected tooth at risk of fracture — clinical urgency.
- **Root canal without crown (4-8 weeks)**: URGENT (score 80-90). Tooth still at risk.
- **Extraction (2-3 months post)**: Ready for implant consult (score 70+). High revenue opportunity.
- **Orthodontics (active, missed monthly)**: URGENT (score 85+). Treatment delays extend total time and cost.
- **Implant post-surgery follow-up missed**: HIGH (score 80+). Implant at risk of failure.
- **Whitening (6-12 months since)**: MODERATE (score 50-65). Good for seasonal campaigns.
- **Single visit, consultation only**: LOW-MODERATE (score 35-50). Unknown loyalty, may need incentive.
